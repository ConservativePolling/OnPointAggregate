1st Session S. 88

NC FARM DEFENSE BILL

SPONSOR: SEN. JAYDEN DAVIS (R-NC)

§ SECTION 1. DEATH TAX REPEAL. Permanently repeals the estate tax for family-owned agricultural operations, ensuring that North Carolina farms stay in the family, not the hands of the IRS.
